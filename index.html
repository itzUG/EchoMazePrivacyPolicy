Overview

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to Echo Maze, an Android game published under the package name com.utkar.echomaze. By installing or playing Echo Maze, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Echo Maze is built to avoid collecting unnecessary personal information. The game does not require user registration, does not request location access, and does not access contacts, photos, camera, or microphone.

Information We Use

Echo Maze does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information. Do not submit personal details through support requests unless necessary.

Permissions

3. Android permissions

  • Internet — Used for optional AI-generated in-game text when a Gemini API key is configured. If the AI request fails, Echo Maze uses built-in fallback messages.
  • Vibration — Used for gameplay feedback such as pulse and impact effects.

Echo Maze does not request permission for location, contacts, SMS, phone, camera, microphone, or external media storage.

Third Parties

4. Third-party services

Echo Maze may use Google Gemini through the Google Generative AI SDK to create short atmospheric text for gameplay. The request is limited to game context such as the level number and a short generation instruction. The app should not send your name, contact details, location, or other personal information for this feature.

Third-party services may process technical information necessary to provide their service, such as IP address, device metadata, or request logs. Their handling of data is governed by their own privacy terms.

Sharing

5. How information is shared

Echo Maze does not sell player data. Information may be shared only in these limited situations:

  • With service providers needed to operate app features, such as AI text generation.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or a valid government request.
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of players, the developer, or others.

Security

6. Data retention and security

Locally stored game progress remains on your device unless you clear the app data or uninstall the app. Echo Maze uses reasonable technical measures to limit data use to what the game needs, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

Children

7. Children's privacy

Echo Maze is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a support request, contact the developer so it can be reviewed and deleted where appropriate.

Choices

8. Your choices

  • You can stop all app data processing by uninstalling Echo Maze.
  • You can delete local progress by clearing the app's storage in Android settings.
  • You can disable vibration using system settings if your device supports it.
  • You can contact the developer with privacy questions or deletion requests.

Changes

9. Updates to this policy

This policy may be updated when Echo Maze changes its features, permissions, service providers, or legal requirements. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when material changes are made.
