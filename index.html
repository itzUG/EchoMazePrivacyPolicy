Overview

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to Echo Maze, an Android game published under the package name com.utkar.echomaze. By installing or playing Echo Maze, you agree to the practices described here.

Echo Maze is built to avoid collecting unnecessary personal information. The game does not require user registration, does not request location access, and does not access contacts, photos, camera, or microphone.

Information We Use

2. Data Stored or Processed

Data Purpose Stored
Game progress (level, update time) Resume gameplay and preserve progress Locally on your device
Gameplay state during session Run score, lives, sound, and controls On device while playing
AI prompt context (current level number) Generate short atmospheric messages when AI is enabled Processed by AI provider only on request

Echo Maze does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information. Do not submit personal information through support requests unless necessary for your inquiry.

Permissions

3. Android Permissions

  • Internet: Used for optional AI-generated in-game text when a Gemini API key is configured. If the request fails, Echo Maze uses built-in fallback messages.
  • Vibration: Used for gameplay feedback, including pulse and impact effects.

Echo Maze does not request permission for location, contacts, SMS, phone, camera, microphone, or external media storage.

Third Parties

4. Third-Party Services

Echo Maze may use Google Gemini through the Google Generative AI SDK to create short atmospheric text for gameplay. The request is limited to game context — such as the level number and a short generation instruction. Your name, contact details, location, or other personal information is never sent for this feature.

Third-party services may process technical information necessary to deliver their service, such as IP address, device metadata, or request logs. Their handling is governed by their own privacy terms.

Sharing

5. How Information Is Shared

Echo Maze does not sell player data. Information may be shared only in these limited situations:

  • With service providers needed to operate app features, such as AI text generation.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or a valid government request.
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of players, the developer, or others.

Security

6. Data Retention & Security

Locally stored game progress remains on your device unless you clear app data or uninstall. Echo Maze uses reasonable technical measures to limit data use to what the game needs, though no method of storage or transmission is entirely secure.

Children

7. Children's Privacy

Echo Maze is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a support request, contact the developer so it can be reviewed and deleted where appropriate.

Choices

8. Your Choices

  • Stop all app data processing by uninstalling Echo Maze.
  • Delete local progress by clearing the app's storage in Android Settings → Apps → Echo Maze → Storage.
  • Disable vibration using system settings if your device supports it.
  • Contact the developer with privacy questions or deletion requests.

Changes

9. Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated when Echo Maze changes its features, permissions, service providers, or legal requirements. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect any material changes.
